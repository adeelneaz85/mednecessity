---

## Deploy to Vercel (5 minutes, free)

### Step 1 — Create a Vercel account
- Go to: https://vercel.com
- Click "Sign Up" — use Google or GitHub account (free)

### Step 2 — Upload this folder
- Once logged in, click **"Add New Project"**
- Click **"Browse"** or drag the entire `mednecessity` folder into Vercel
- Vercel will auto-detect it as a React app

### Step 3 — Deploy
- Click **"Deploy"**
- Wait about 2 minutes while Vercel builds it
- You will get a live URL like: `https://mednecessity-ai.vercel.app`

### Step 4 — Share
- Copy the URL Vercel gives you
- Open it in any browser — the app is live!
- Share with your back office team
